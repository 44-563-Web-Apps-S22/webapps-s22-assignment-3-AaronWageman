New Mascot is the Wilmington Wolf

Our local minor league baseball team here in Wilmington, NC has decided on a new mascot. Our mascot is white and loves baseball. The new mascot is an Arctic Wolf. The mascots name is the Wilmington Wolf

Fun facts about the Wilmington Wolf

  • He loves the warmth of North Carolina
  • He howls after every home run
  • He loves to eat bacon
